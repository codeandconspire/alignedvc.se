Our approach

We seek alignment with founders and their mission.

As well as building relationships that are supportive as well as successful. We leverage our community of founders, investors, extended network and experience to support your business with what it needs to flourish. Making it a true journey of learning and support.

Our office is in Stockholm but we spend our time wherever the inspiring people and ideas may be.

Before we were called Aligned, you might have heard of us as 183 Invest. Same company, same team.

What we invest in

Founders that avoid the most trodden path.

Finding unconventional ways to lead, serve, define, change, and innovate is a natural way forward for you as an entrepreneur.

We are sector agnostic and invest in businesses in a wide range of industries – from Ad-tech to consumer goods, ecommerce to property development.

Who we are

Aligned is an investment vehicle sprung out of the joy for entrepreneurship. We also believe it is possible, as an investor, to promote positive change not only for companies but for the broader community.

Aligned was founded in 2019 by Eric Carlson Rydman and Christian af Jochnick, two friends who grew up together.

Eric Carlson Rydman

Managing Director

Eric handles the day-to-day operations of aligned, supporting the portfolio and scouting for new investments.

Before aligned, Eric held senior commercial and strategic positions at Spotify. Prior to that, he was a Corporate Finance Advisor specialising in M&A at Stockholm-based Access Partners.

Christian af Jochnick

Founding Partner

Following a start of the career at Goldman Sachs in London followed by a start-up journey as a co-founder, Christian entered the venture space and has been supporting entrepreneurs since 2013.

He’s supporting a range of social and environmental initiatives as a board member of the Af Jochnick Foundation. His main personal focus is the responsible generative agricultural ecosystem.
